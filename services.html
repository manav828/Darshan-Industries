Common Questions

What industries do you primarily serve? expand_more

We support a wide range of manufacturers, with core strengths in Pharmaceuticals, Textile Processing, Paints & Coatings, Plastic Manufacturing, and Food Processing industries.

Do you handle international compliance and tariffs? expand_more

Yes, our import/export division manages all legal documentation, customs clearance, duty payments, and international trade compliance as part of our turnkey sourcing service.

What are your typical lead times for bulk orders? expand_more

Lead times vary by material origin and shipping method. Domestic orders typically take 3-7 days, while international imports range from 4 to 12 weeks depending on port logistics and availability.
